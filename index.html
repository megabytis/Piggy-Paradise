How to Play
  1. 🎲 Player 1 starts - Roll the dice to add its value to your current and total score
  2. 🔄 Alternate turns - After each roll, the turn automatically passes to the other player
  3. ☠️ Rolling a 1 - You lose just that turn's chance (no score penalty), and the turn passes
  4. 💾 HOLD anytime - Keep your accumulated score and voluntarily pass the turn
  5. 🏆 Winning - First player to reach the target score (default: 50) wins instantly!
  6. ⚠️ Note - Scores are cumulative - each roll adds to both current and total scores
